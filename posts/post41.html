ATS 2025 Wrap-Up — What an Experience!

✈️ Just got back from ATS 2025 in San Francisco — what an incredible experience!

This year's conference was a reminder of why I love the intersection of clinical science and computational biology. A few highlights:

🧬 Saw cutting-edge single-cell work on lung disease that will shape our next experiments
🤝 Connected with researchers from around the world who share our passion
📊 Presented our work and got valuable feedback that will improve our analyses
🌉 San Francisco itself was amazing — the energy of the city matched the conference

What struck me most: the growing recognition that bioinformatics is no longer optional in lung research. Single-cell RNA-seq is becoming the standard.

Huge thanks to my collaborators, mentors, and everyone who stopped by our poster. The conversations we had will fuel our work for months.

Until next year! 🙌
