Two free tools — doyouMind and DualScreen — combined into one workspace.

doyouMind: a free, forget-proof mind mapping tool for research papers, ideas, lab work, grocery lists — anything. It evolves dynamically based on feedback. No friction. No limitations.

DualScreen: a multi-monitor synchronization tool that merges multiple screens and syncs files (HTML, images) or displays websites side by side.

Combined: a mind map displays on one screen while your work content appears on another — fully synchronized. A living, breathing workspace.

Key features:
• Feedback-driven development — rapid changes based on user input
• Phone and desktop compatibility
• Optional personal Zoom walkthroughs
• Open-source approach
