Getting your job fully done by AI and getting it done with AI under your supervision are two very different things.

The first is like winning the lottery. You get the money—but you don't understand how hard it is to earn, so you spend it recklessly, often with little long-term benefit.

The second is earning less than the lottery winner, but benefiting from every single part of it. You know what you're doing. You understand the process. You walk forward with solid, deliberate steps.

Same tools. Very different outcomes.

You choose your path. 🙂
