Science Is a Dynamic Journey — Be Open to New Hypotheses

🔬 Science isn't about building a hypothesis and proving it right at all costs. It's a dynamic journey; a continuous search for the best path toward understanding. You can't just try one route, declare it "the way," and stop exploring. True scientific growth happens when you acknowledge that your route might not be the correct one and have the courage to pivot, test new directions, and refine your approach. That's not failure. That's the essence of discovery.
