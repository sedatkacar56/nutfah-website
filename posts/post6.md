🔬 #HiddenGemsInSeurat #3: Exploring #LinkedDimPlot() – Interactive Spatial & UMAP Visualization! When analyzing spatial transcriptomics, we often need to compare Seurat's unsupervised clusters with their spatial locations. Seeing where the same clusters appear in both UMAP/tSNE and spatial coordinates can provide crucial biological insights.

💡 LinkedDimPlot() is just for you! With LinkedDimPlot(), you can:

✔️ Interactively explore spatial & clustering plots side by side.
✔️ Locate specific cells on the spatial image and see their cluster in DimPlot().
✔️ Identify spatial regions where the same clusters appear.
✔️ Click on points to instantly highlight corresponding cells in both views.

Left: A spatial plot with bins representing spatially captured transcriptomics data.
Right: The same clusters from DimPlot(), colored identically to match the spatial view.

✨ Want to explore gene expression instead? Try #LinkedFeaturePlot(), which lets you visualize specific gene expression in both spatial and UMAP plots!
