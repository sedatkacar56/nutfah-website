Analyzing Over 1 Million Single Cells from PAH Patients — Parse Biosciences + BPCells + Seurat v5

🧬 We're analyzing over 1 million single cells from 9 human peripheral blood samples — 5 healthy controls and 4 PAH (pulmonary arterial hypertension) patients — using Parse Biosciences combined with a BPCells-backed Seurat v5 pipeline.

BPCells enables on-disk computation of matrices that would otherwise exhaust RAM at this scale. Seurat v5 natively supports BPCells-backed objects — allowing the full analysis pipeline (normalization, integration, clustering, annotation) to run without loading the full matrix into memory.

The research goal: identify immune cell populations, transcriptional changes, and potential drug targets in pulmonary arterial hypertension.

1 million cells. 9 samples. On-disk computation. The tools now exist to make this feasible on standard research hardware.
