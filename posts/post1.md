Don't rush insistently to secure an industry job as a Ph.D. You are not merely seeking any industrial position where you showcase yourself or earn a decent salary, are you? You have great skills and have only one life to live. And each passing day is precious. Once you find your job it will consume a significant amount of your time. You would not want to spend your time on something that you are not genuinely into, are you? Take your time - Don't rush! Be discerning, not based on your skills, but on the vision you have for your future, where you want to see improvement in yourself. Lack of expertise in a specific area shouldn't deter you; you have the capability. You are invaluable. Upon securing a job, you may become one of the millions of employees in the company, but this job will play a significant role in shaping your future steps. Do not worry about none of your hundreds of applications that have been accepted. Value yourself. Improve yourself. Savor the freshness of each new day. There are millions of opportunities to take! Countless opportunities await—millions of areas where you can enhance yourself and become the person you aspire to be.
