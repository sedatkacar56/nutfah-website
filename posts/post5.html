FindSubCluster: Targeted Subclustering in Seurat

The post introduces `FindSubCluster()`, a Seurat function for targeted subclustering. While `FindClusters()` identifies broad cell populations, `FindSubCluster()` enables deeper analysis of specific clusters without globally increasing resolution. Applies subclustering only to clusters of interest (e.g., Cluster 0), creates meaningful subdivisions (0_0, 0_1, 0_2) while preserving other data. Advantages include granular analysis without over-fragmentation and improved cell-type annotation.
