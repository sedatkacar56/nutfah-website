Understanding Algorithms: Go Beyond Just Using Tools

For those of us coming from a wet lab background, it's time to go beyond just using bioinformatics tools—we need to understand the algorithms behind them!

Why It Matters

Techniques like PCA (Principal Component Analysis), UMAP, t-SNE, K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), clustering algorithms, and machine learning models are widely used in modern biology, from single-cell transcriptomics to structural biology.

But blindly trusting these methods in every analysis without questioning their assumptions might limit our ability to uncover something truly novel.

Key Benefits

  • Identifying data biases
  • Informed algorithm selection
  • Avoiding default parameter over-reliance
  • Enabling truly novel discoveries
