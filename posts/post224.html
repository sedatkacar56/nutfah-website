You Should Dig and Learn Yourself — Knowledge Undiscovered Is the Same as Nonexistent

You should dig and learn yourself. Knowledge is somewhere — you should find it, discover it. Nobody is going to show you. You are not a baby.

Many times when I dive deep into single cell, I realize such amazing things and ask myself: why did nobody tell me? Because they don't have to. Or maybe they did not see it yet. It is on you to find it out.

The knowledge existing somewhere far away from you does not help you. It is the same thing as not discovered for you. As long as you know it and apply it, then it is useful.

Keep diving deep. Contemplate. Reflect. Don't leave yourself in the arms of anything. Don't be dependent — make them dependent.

Have a blast weekend! 🌱
