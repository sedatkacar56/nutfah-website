Automating Repetitive Metadata Submissions for GEO and SRA

🧠 What is FillaZilla? If you've ever stared at a form submission portal wondering which field is going to throw an error next... you're not alone. Enter FillaZilla — a sleek, time-saving tool designed to streamline form-filling and auto-complete tasks, especially when dealing with repetitive submissions like FASTQ metadata or transcriptomics sample sheets.

🔍 So, what exactly is it? FillaZilla is a lightweight form-filling utility that can auto-populate structured forms using predefined templates or CSV files. Think of it as your submission sidekick for GEO, SRA, or any system that makes you repeat yourself again and again.

⚡ Why use it?
⏱️ Save time – Say goodbye to copying/pasting 50+ mouse sample names.
🧼 Reduce errors – Pre-fill with clean, consistent data.
🧠 Less brain fatigue – Focus on the science, not the submission UI.
🔄 Batch-friendly – Great for handling 10, 100, or 1,000 entries.

✅ Whether you're submitting metadata for a lung endothelial cell atlas, running through a spatial transcriptomics pipeline, or prepping a GEO accession — tools like FillaZilla can boost accuracy, reduce grunt work, and keep you in flow.
