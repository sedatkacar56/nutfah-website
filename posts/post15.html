Linux Shell vs RStudio: Two Different Worlds

The Metaphor

Linux shell work is like driving a truck—powerful, but no rear window. You trust the engine, not visuals.

RStudio is like driving a car with big rear windows—everything's visual: plots, tables, observable objects, and global items.

Both Have Their Place

Linux shell gives you raw power and direct control. RStudio gives you visibility and instant feedback. The best data scientists are comfortable switching between both depending on the task at hand.
