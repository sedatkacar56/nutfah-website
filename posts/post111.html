-r vs -R in Linux: Does It Follow Symbolic Links?

Do you know the difference between the flag -r and the flag -R? 🤔

I used to think it's just that one is capital and the other isn't... but that single letter actually changes how deep your command travels! ⚙️

In Linux, these are called flags — little switches that change what a command does.

Example:

grep -r "keyword" .
grep -R "keyword" .

Both mean recursive search, but there's a hidden twist:

Flag Meaning / Follows normal folders / Follows symbolic links
-r Recursive ✅ Yes ❌ No
-R Recursive (follow links) ✅ Yes ✅ Yes

So basically — -r goes into real folders only. -R dives into everything, even shortcut (linked) folders.

Such a tiny detail, but it decides how far your search really goes. 🔍
