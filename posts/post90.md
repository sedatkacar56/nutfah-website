🚀 NEXTFLOW: Turning Single Cell Pipelines into Reproducible Workflows

🔹 Without Nextflow
Manual setup for each sample or experiment. Error recovery is painful and failed runs often mean starting over. Difficult to reproduce and configurations scattered across scripts. Scaling challenges and running many datasets becomes messy.

🔹 With Nextflow
Profiles & configuration system → easily switch between local, HPC, or cloud environments. Automated orchestration → each analysis runs with clearly defined resources and isolation. Work directories & logs → every step is tracked, with transparent inputs, outputs, and logs. Reproducibility by design → runs can be repeated or shared with the same settings. Scalability → one workflow definition handles multiple datasets consistently.

✅ The Difference
The biological outputs remain the same. The difference lies in how workflows are managed: Nextflow ensures analyses are robust, reproducible, and scalable; qualities essential in modern data-driven biology.
