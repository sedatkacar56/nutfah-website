🧬 Nutfah Single-cell Omics has released a free Figure Builder tool — a browser-based solution for assembling publication-ready manuscript figures.

What it does:
• Upload multiple plots, images, and microscopy data
• Resize and arrange panels flexibly
• Auto-label panels (A, B, C, D, etc.)
• Add legends and captions
• Export high-resolution figures

No software to install. No cost. No catch. Just open it in your browser.

Available at: nutfahsinglecellomics.com/figure-panel-builder.html

Built by scientists, for scientists. The tool will improve based on community feedback — if something is wrong or missing, it gets fixed quickly.

Designed specifically for researchers working with single-cell data, bioinformatics, scRNAseq, spatial transcriptomics, and genomics who spend too much time manually formatting figures for publication.
