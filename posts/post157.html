Domain Knowledge Is Your Superpower in Bioinformatics

🧠 Domain knowledge is your superpower in bioinformatics — don't overlook it.

Yes, AI can help you write code. Yes, you don't need to write every chunk manually. Sketch your idea, draft something rough, and let AI expand it. Then supervise it, correct it, and refine it.

But remember — the real challenge isn't writing a single script. It's knowing how to combine those chunks into a full pipeline that makes sense for your biological question.

And that's where domain knowledge matters. You need to understand what's biologically meaningful, when results are trustworthy, and how to troubleshoot when they're not.

🧬 Got strange results? You need to ask the right questions to the wet lab scientists.
🧪 Got clean outputs? You still need to judge if they make sense in the context of your system.

Use AI wisely. Let it speed you up — but never skip the thinking. Your brain is still the engine. AI is just a very powerful assistant.
