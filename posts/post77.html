Certificate of Appreciation from SHtSBiB 2025

Grateful to receive a Certificate of Appreciation from SHtSBiB 2025 for my talk, "Let Your Passion Shape You: My Journey to Single-Cell RNA Sequencing". In my presentation, I shared how passion in science can emerge through exploration, from my early wet-lab work to leading single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) data analysis, and how combining scRNA seq with spatial transcriptomics enables us to pinpoint cells in their exact tissue context. The talk took place at 11:30 PM EST, a late night, but absolutely worth it for the chance to share insights with a global audience. A big thank you to the organizers! 🔬
