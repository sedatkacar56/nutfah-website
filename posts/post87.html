droplevels() to Remove Unused Factor Levels in Seurat

Did you see in your newly subset single cell data table that "a group with no value" at all EXIST?

table(seurat_obj$group)
Control  Hypoxia  Treatment
   7000     7500          0

Ok. Do not panic. Everything is fine... just do this..

seurat_obj$group <- droplevels(seurat_obj$group)
table(seurat_obj$group)
Control  Hypoxia
   7000     7500

Droplevels function helps you to discard unused factor levels! "0" value group gone! Easy peasy
