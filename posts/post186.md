What if one click could open a random Qur'an verse for you to reflect on? I built a small tool that does exactly that.

📖 Quran Explorer lets you:
→ Discover any of the 6,236 verses instantly
→ Read the Arabic in Uthmani script
→ Hover over each word to see its meaning
→ See the Surah name, verse number & theme

A Turkish version (Kuran Kaşifi) is also available — with Diyanet translation and Turkish word meanings.

Link of this small App is in the comments. Don't forget to try the it!!!
