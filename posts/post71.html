H-1B Visas and U.S. College Grads — A Closer Look

🚨 H-1B Visas & U.S. College Grads: A Closer Look A recent Newsweek article highlights growing concerns that the H-1B visa program, designed to bring in highly skilled foreign workers, may be displacing recent U.S. college graduates—especially in tech fields. Critics argue that some companies use the program to cut costs, favoring visa holders over domestic talent. Meanwhile, supporters claim it fills crucial skill gaps in areas where the U.S. lacks trained professionals. The debate is intensifying amid layoffs and a tight job market for new grads.
