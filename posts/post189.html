US States Map Quiz — A Fun Side Project

I realized that i dont know the locations of statesof US on map. I built a little side project this week - a US States Map Quiz 🗺️ - and try to play it couple of time with my family to get used to it!

Two modes: 🟡 Mode 1: A state lights up on the map → you guess the name from multiple options. 📍 Mode 2: A state name appears on top → you tap the right state on the map.

-Wrong answers stay RED and correct ones stay GREEN as you go, so by the end of the quiz the map tells the whole story of how you did.
