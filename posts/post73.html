You Cannot Imagine How Important It Is to Like Your Job

You cannot imagine how important it is to like your job and what you do...

You cannot imagine the strength of motivation and passion...

You cannot imagine how important it is to look forward to the future with hope, confidence, and awareness of your capabilities...

You cannot imagine how important it is to know that Someone beyond you is with you — as with all human beings whose hearts are not closed...

It is easy to say, "If you don't like something, just quit" — as if you don't need to continue your job to survive and bring food to your family… as if you are alone and without responsibilities…

But I wanna add a BIG high capital BUT here:

BUT...

You gotta do something. You gotta take baby steps. One step at a time... one... two... three...

Do what you like. When you do something you enjoy, it's like playing chess, chatting with friends… Everyone likes something and has tendencies. Find yours. Spend a little time to search for it. Learn one thing at a time. At least talk about it with your friends. Believe me — this alone gives you hope to reach your dream job and makes you feel more energetic. It opens your motivation gates.

You won't do everything at once. You'll step in. Make the first step — believe me, the rest will come along. You'll start seeing things through the lens of your dream. You'll relate everything to it — like how search engines show you what you're interested in. Life will do the same, with the will of the All-Almighty.

First step. Don't forget. Lie down on a couch right now and contemplate. You do your part — and trust in God for the big, complicated, never-guessed part. But do your part, which is the gateway to the second part

#StepByStep #PassionDriven #LifePurpose #FaithAndAction #SmallStepsBigChange
