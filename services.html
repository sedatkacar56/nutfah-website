Services

End-to-end bioinformatics analysis tailored to your data and publication goals.

🧬 Single-Cell RNA-seq (scRNA-seq)

$4,000 – $8,000

Full pipeline analysis from raw Seurat/AnnData object or FASTQ files to final publication-ready deliverables.

  • Quality control and preprocessing
  • Dimensionality reduction (PCA, UMAP)
  • Cell clustering and cell type annotation (manual + SingleR/Azimuth)
  • Subpopulation identification and abundance analysis
  • Differential expression (DEG) analysis between conditions
  • Module scoring (UCell — inflammatory, stress, functional signatures)
  • Trajectory inference (Monocle3)
  • Pathway enrichment (KEGG, GO)

Deliverables: Publication-ready figures and DEG tables. CellChat and SCENIC available as add-on services.

🗺️ Spatial Transcriptomics

$5,000 – $10,000

Spatial gene expression analysis that maps cell types and gene activity directly onto tissue sections.

  • 10x Visium and Stereo-seq analysis
  • Spatial clustering and cell type deconvolution
  • Spatially variable gene identification
  • Integration with scRNA-seq reference data
  • Tissue domain segmentation
  • Publication-ready spatial maps

Deliverables: Publication-ready spatial figures and gene expression maps with written biological interpretation.

📊 Bulk RNA-seq Reanalysis

$1,500 – $3,500

Rigorous reanalysis of existing bulk RNA-seq data with corrected statistical approaches and publication-ready figures.

  • DEG analysis with DESeq2 or edgeR
  • Pathway enrichment (KEGG, GO, GSEA)
  • Contamination analysis (cell-type specific gene filtering)
  • Heatmaps, volcano plots, PCA plots
  • Written results summary

Deliverables: Publication-ready figures, DEG tables, and written biological summary.
